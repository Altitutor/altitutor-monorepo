QUANTITATIVE REASONING PRACTICE QUESTIONS

 

This question bank contains:

 

   • Question Bank Section 1 – Quantitative Reasoning

   • Question Bank Section 2 – Quantitative Reasoning

   • Question Bank Section 3 – Quantitative Reasoning

   • Question Bank Section 4 – Quantitative Reasoning

 

The ‘Navigator’ function at the bottom right of the screen allows you to navigate between questions and sections within the bank.

 

The Quantitative Reasoning subtest assesses your ability to use numerical skills to solve problems. Questions are less to do with numerical facility and more to do with problem solving.

 

You are presented with questions that most often refer to charts and graphs containing data.

 

Most questions are shown as sets of four questions each connected to the same data. There are some questions that standalone and do not share data.

 

Each question has five answer options. You are only able to select one response.

 

An onscreen calculator is available to assist you with this subtest – you can access this by clicking on the button at the top left of the screen.

 

The calculator can be operated by using the mouse or the number pad on the keyboard.

 

The calculator opens in a separate window to the test screen. If you click off the calculator window back to the test screen (or use a keyboard shortcut, for example Alt + F to Flag for Review) the calculator will not function until you click back on it.

 

Troubleshooting tips:

 

   • Ensure that 'Num Lock’ is on for the number pad to work.

   • Most calculator issues can be resolved by clicking the ON/C button or closing the calculator and relaunching.

 

If you are sitting your test at a test centre, you will be given a laminated notebook (A4 in size) and a pen for making notes.
